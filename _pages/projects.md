## Current Research Projects

### Geospatial Machine Learning
Developing scalable ML models for land-use/land-cover mapping, crop type classification, building density estimation, and environmental change detection using satellite and aerial imagery. This includes our work on local vs. global models for higher quality LULC maps, zero-shot geospatial classification, and optimizing data pipelines for Earth observation.

[Project page at Microsoft Research](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/project/geospatial-machine-learning/)

### AI for Health
Leveraging machine learning to forecast childhood malnutrition, support maternal and child health decision-making, and bridge the gap between data science and domain expertise in public health systems across sub-Saharan Africa.

[Project page at Microsoft Research](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/project/ai-for-health/)

### AI for Cultural Heritage
Using satellite imagery and deep learning to detect looted archaeological sites, contributing to the protection of cultural heritage around the world.

### Advance Sustainability – AI for Good
Building AI solutions that contribute to environmental sustainability, including monitoring smallholder irrigation expansion, tracking adoption of sustainable farming practices, and analyzing decades-long environmental changes.

[Project page at Microsoft Research](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/project/advance-sustainability-ai-for-good/)

### Fundamental Rights – AI for Good
Addressing fundamental rights challenges through AI, with focus on trustworthy AI, fairness, and out-of-distribution detection in machine learning systems.

[Project page at Microsoft Research](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/project/fundamental-rights-ai-for-good/)
